About Me

I am Gage Sampson, an entrepreneur, marketer, and co-founder of TikTok Titans, a TikTok Shop Partnered Agency that has generated over $5 million in GMV for brands while helping hundreds of creators monetize their content.

I grew up in Franklin Furnace, Ohio, a small town of about 1,500 people, as one of seven siblings. My dad was a barber who owned a shop in Willardburg, Ohio, and following in his footsteps I started my first career in barbering. Along the way, I discovered the power of digital platforms when a random skit I posted on TikTok hit 10 million views in 24 hours and grew my account to 230,000 followers in just a month.

Those early experiences shaped my approach to business: blending creativity, sales psychology, and scalable systems. Today at TikTok Titans, I work with brands like Dude Wipes, Goli, Dr. Biocare, Selerb, Nello Supercalm, and NeuroGum, while also coaching creators through the Titans Inner Circle. Whether it is running high performing campaigns, leveraging AI to create new tools, or helping creators secure brand deals, my mission is simple: help creators win, help brands grow, and keep building the future of digital marketing.

My Story

Add your image here

I grew up in Franklin Furnace, Ohio, a very small town with just around 1,500 people. As one of seven siblings, five brothers and one sister, I went to Green Local School District. Sports were everything. Basketball and football were not just hobbies, they were identity, and I earned All State honors in basketball. But even while chasing wins on the court, I always had one thing clear in my mind: I wanted to make money.

After high school, I was pushed toward barbering because my dad was a barber and owned a barber shop in Willardburg, Ohio. Following in his footsteps, I went to barber school and started my first career cutting hair.

In 2020, I posted a random skit video on TikTok, and within 24 hours it exploded. It got 10 million views, which led to more viral skits, and by the end of the month I had grown my account to about 230,000 followers. TikTok was not monetizable then, so I returned to the barber chair. But the spark for digital growth never left.

By 2023, that spark turned into fire. I hit the road during Taylor Swift's Eras Tour, selling bracelets as a traveling vendor with my best friend. That experience taught me a huge lesson in sales: just because I would not buy something does not mean no one else will. There is a market out there for almost anything, and as long as you know how to sell it, you can make money.

Then in early 2024, I saw TikTok Shop emerging, knew it was the future of e-commerce, and a few months later co-founded TikTok Titans. Ever since, we have been scaling creators and brands to entirely new levels.
