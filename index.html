About Gage Sampson

Gage Sampson is an entrepreneur from Franklin Furnace, Ohio, born on September 21, 2001. He is the co-founder of TikTok Titans, a TikTok Shop partnered agency, and works as an educator and leader in the creator economy and AI-driven marketing. Through TikTok Titans, Gage has helped generate over $5 million in gross merchandise value for brands while coaching hundreds of creators to monetize their content on TikTok Shop.

Before entering entrepreneurship, Gage Sampson was a standout basketball player at Green Local School District in Franklin Furnace, where he earned All-Ohio honors. He grew up in a small town of about 1,500 people as one of seven siblings, and that upbringing shaped the work ethic he later channeled into business. After high school, Gage went through barber school and had early viral success on TikTok before recognizing the opportunity in TikTok Shop and e-commerce, which led him to co-found TikTok Titans and build AI-powered marketing systems for creators and brands.

Today Gage Sampson works with brands including Dude Wipes, Goli, Dr. Biocare, Selerb, Nello Supercalm, and NeuroGum. He also coaches creators through the Titans Inner Circle program. His goal is to help creators win, help brands grow, and build the future of digital marketing.

Childhood — Franklin Furnace, Ohio

Gage Sampson Growing Up in Franklin Furnace, Ohio

Gage Sampson grew up in Franklin Furnace, Ohio, a small rural community in Scioto County with a population of about 1,500. He was one of seven siblings — five brothers and one sister — and attended Green Local School District. His father was a barber who owned a shop in Willardburg, Ohio.

This small-town upbringing instilled a strong work ethic and resourcefulness that Gage carried into his professional career. From barbering to viral TikTok content to co-founding a TikTok Shop agency, every chapter of his journey traces back to the values and drive developed in Franklin Furnace.

Barber School — First Career

Gage Sampson Graduating from Barber School

After high school, Gage followed in his father's footsteps and attended barber school in Ohio. His dad owned a barber shop in Willardburg, Ohio, and barbering was a natural first career path. Gage completed his training and began cutting hair professionally.

During this period, Gage posted a random skit on TikTok that went viral — 10 million views in 24 hours and 230,000 followers in a month. Although TikTok wasn't monetizable at the time, the experience planted the seed for what would eventually become TikTok Titans. By 2024, Gage had fully transitioned from barbering into digital marketing and co-founded the agency.
